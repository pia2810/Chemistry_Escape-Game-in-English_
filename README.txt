Chemistry Escape Room App
=========================

Start:
Open index.html in a browser.

Recommended use:
- One device per team or one central projected device.
- Duration: 90 minutes.
- Station time: about 6-7 minutes per station plus final discussion.
- Final code: LAB SAFETY FIRST UNLOCK THE YEAR REPORT WITH SAFE METHODS.

Teacher area:
Use the "Teacher key" button in the left sidebar for solutions and timing notes.

Topics:
- Nuclide notation and particle numbers
- Isotopes, nuclides and atomic mass unit
- Rutherford, Bohr and orbital model
- PSE groups, periods and trends
- Ions, noble gas configuration and salt formulas
- Covalent, ionic and metallic bonding
- Molecules and molecular substances
- Intermolecular forces
- Safety in the laboratory
- Glassware, accuracy and final lab decision

Offline use:
The app uses only local files. No internet connection is required.
